How It Works

From signup to sourcing
in 4 simple steps

Sellvro removes friction from every stage of B2B procurement.

01

Create Your Account

Register as a buyer or supplier. Provide your business details for platform verification — typically completed within 24 hours.

02

Discover & Compare

Browse verified product catalogs. Filter by category, region, price range, and supplier rating to find exactly what your business needs.

03

Place Your Order

Add to cart, fund your Sellvro wallet, and place your order. Our platform manages the supplier coordination and fulfillment workflow automatically.

04

Track & Receive

Monitor order status in real time from your dashboard. Get notified at every stage until your goods are delivered and confirmed.
